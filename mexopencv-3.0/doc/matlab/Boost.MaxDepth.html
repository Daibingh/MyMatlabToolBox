cv.Boost/MaxDepth

The maximum possible depth of the tree.

That is the training algorithms attempts to split a node while its depth is less than MaxDepth. The root node has zero depth. The actual depth may be smaller if the other termination criteria are met (see the outline of the training procedure here), and/or if the tree is pruned. Default value is 1.
